Interpretation

What this dashboard reports

Time-domain HRV

Mean NN, SDNN, RMSSD, pNN50, and heart rate are estimated from detected RR intervals.

Frequency-domain HRV

LF and HF band power are derived from a uniformly resampled RR series using a direct PSD estimate.

Nonlinear behavior

Poincare-derived SD1 and SD2 provide a compact view of short-term and long-term interval dispersion.

Important note

This is an educational dashboard and not a diagnostic medical device. Always validate against clinical workflows.
